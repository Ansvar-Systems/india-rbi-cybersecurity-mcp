## What's NOT Included

| Gap | Reason | Planned? |
|-----|--------|----------|
| RBI enforcement orders and penalties | Not systematically published | No |
| NBFC-specific IT circulars (sub-threshold) | Scope limited to major REs | Yes v2 |
| Regional Rural Bank IT directions | Separate guidance, lower complexity | No |
| SEBI/IRDAI/NPCI cybersecurity frameworks | Out of scope for this MCP | No |
| Hindi-language publications | English focus for v1 | Yes v2 |

## Limitations

- The RBI notifications portal uses ASP.NET; some content requires JavaScript rendering
- Circular coverage focuses on IT/cybersecurity topics; general banking circulars excluded
- Some older master directions (pre-2016) may not be fully indexed
- Reference numbers in the seeded database are representative; actual RBI circular IDs vary
